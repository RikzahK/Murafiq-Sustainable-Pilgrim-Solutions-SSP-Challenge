## Sustainable Solutions for Pilgrims (SSP) Challenge

**Murafiq** was developed for the **Sustainable Solutions for Pilgrims (SSP) Challenge**, a global innovation initiative led by the **Saudi Ministry of Hajj and Umrah**.

The challenge brings together students, entrepreneurs, startups, and innovators worldwide to design scalable and sustainable technological solutions that enhance the Hajj and Umrah experience while addressing environmental and logistical challenges faced by millions of pilgrims annually.

Murafiq successfully qualified for the **International Saudi Evaluation Round**, demonstrating strong real-world applicability toward future smart pilgrimage ecosystems.

---

## Project Overview

Murafiq is an intelligent pilgrimage support ecosystem designed to improve safety, mobility, and operational efficiency using Artificial Intelligence, IoT infrastructure, and real-time analytics.

The solution addresses critical pilgrimage challenges including:

- Heat exposure and environmental risks  
- Crowd congestion and safety management  
- Navigation difficulties  
- Language barriers  
- Emergency response coordination  
- Transportation inefficiencies  

Murafiq enables pilgrims, organizers, and volunteers to operate within a unified smart assistance platform.

---

## Vision

To enable a safe, seamless, and spiritually focused pilgrimage experience through intelligent and sustainable technological infrastructure aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.

## Mission

To empower pilgrims with real-time navigation, health monitoring, multilingual assistance, and coordinated emergency response systems that ensure safety, comfort, and accessibility throughout the pilgrimage journey.

## Core Solution Architecture

### AI Cooling and Crowd Management Drones

Autonomous drones equipped with thermal imaging monitor crowd density and environmental conditions in real time.

**Capabilities**
- Detection of heat-intensive zones  
- Automated cooling mist deployment  
- Crowd flow monitoring  
- Predictive congestion analytics  
- Data collection for infrastructure planning  



---

### IoT-Enabled Smart Pilgrim Bracelet

A wearable safety device providing continuous assistance.

**Key Features**
- GPS navigation and tracking  
- Real-time health monitoring  
- Emergency SOS alerts  
- Group coordination support  
- Metro and transportation integration  


---

### Smart Camps in Mina — AI Occupancy Monitoring

AI-based people-counting systems monitor tent occupancy levels to prevent overcrowding.

**Benefits**
- Automated capacity monitoring  
- Real-time overcrowding alerts  
- Improved operational efficiency  
- Historical analytics for planning  


---

### AI-Powered Transportation Intelligence

Smart mobility system providing live transportation insights.

**Functionalities**
- Real-time bus tracking  
- Seat availability monitoring  
- Route optimization  
- Emergency and delay alerts  
- Multilingual accessibility
